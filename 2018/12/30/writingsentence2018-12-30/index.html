Example Sentence

  5. The example is not rare; it is one of many examples I have heard
  6. A recent/new study/investigation taken/done as a factory/university by officials/experts
    suggests/show/proves that
  7. According to the figures/statistics/findings/data/graph/table provided by
  8. According to a recent/new research, it can be seen that

  10. Much available evidence shows that
  11. Examples I heard of help me make the conclusion that
  12. This/It shows/points out the important/obvious fact that
  13. We may/can state/mention/admit/point out the obvious/important/surprising fact that

  4. Although A has much advantage over … it , however ….
  5. A’s advantage sounds ridiculous/ means nothing when B’s advantages are mentioned/considered/taken into consideration
  6. When the advantages and disadvantages of .. are carefully compared, the most obvious finding/conclusion is
  7. ALlthough everyone believes that… I doubt/wonder whether the argument ….
  8. Contrary to widely/commonly/generally held belief/ideas/views. I believe that
  9. Although the popular/nationwide/commonly-accepted belief/idea is that …., a current/new/recent
    study/survey/poll/investigation indicates/shows/suggests that…

  11. They may be right about…, but they seem to fail to mention/consider the fact that

Reason Sentence

  1. Among the most convincing/important reasons given/offered by people for …, one should be stressed/emphasized/mentioned that
  2. One may see/view/think of the trend/increase/phenomenon as a result of…
  3. The increase/change/failure/success in… mainly results from/is because of is due to/owing to the fact that…/ several factors
  4. We may blame/criticize … for …, but the causes for the problem/phenomenon is beyond that
  5. One may owe the increase/decrease/change to …, but … is not by itself an adequate explanation
  6. One may trace this trend/problem back to…, but it does not answer the question of / why …
  7. There are many/different/several/a number of causes/reasons for this important/obvious growth/change/decline/increase in … First, Second, Finally
  8. A number of factors could lead to/result in/influence the change/increase/decrease/success/failure/development in…
  9. Another obvious/important factor/cause of… is …
  10. Also significant is …
  11. Why are/do/did ..? For one thing,…/On reason is …, For another,…/Another is …. Perhaps the most important reason is…
  12. … is also/partly/mainly responsible for the rise/increase/decrease in…
  13. The reasons/causes for … are varied/many and maybe/perhaps they are found in the fact..
  14. Some people state that the problem is with teenagers/students who… But it does not explain../they are only partly right..
  15. It may cause/produce/bring about a significant change/rise in…

  2. By comparison with 1990, it decreased from 10 to 5 percent./dropped to 15 percent./fell by 15 percent.

  4. It has dropped/increased/decreased almost/more than/about two and a half times/twice/six times, compared with
  5. The number/percentage is twice/4 times/half as much as that of 1990
