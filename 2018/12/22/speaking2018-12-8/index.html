Speaking Emergency Package

  2. 保持流利度
  3. 展现词汇量
  4. 展示思路扩展过程

Step 1
When you can not understand please ask for help?(As soon as possible)
I’m sorry, can you repeat/rephrase your question please？
This’s a tough question … I seriously don’t have any idea , can you help me with it please.

说错词/句子后，不要卡单词，重新讲一遍句子
I think learning math is important to children because it can develop their imagination sorry I mean their logic thinking ability.

hesitation/pauses

Step2
Part 2 超难题
Describe a useful skill you learned in a math class from your primary/high school
You should say：
What skills was
How you learned it
Who taught you
And why it was important to you
vibrant（adj.有活力的）
psyched (adj 兴奋的)
special connection

  1. 无论何时都要保持流利度
  2. 如果卡住，就讲下一个点
  3. 但讲下一个点的时候需要点一下题
  4. 如果想起点，可以接回来Oh another thing I’d like to say more
  5. 如果不太会的话，你可以暗示考官自己不会，猜测答案

What methods can be applied to make math more interesting?
Well I didn’t pay much attention to math class when I was in school I have to admit.
But I guess maybe the teacher could start from doing more experiments with students instead of taeching theoretical knowledge
The reason is that it attracts students’s attentions..
But I can’t say for sure though

Step3
What kinds historical characters are important to people in your country?

关键词： historical characters/ important /Chinese people
展现思考过程： 拖延时间+1. 观点-解释观点： 2. 例子-引申出观点

Well that’s quite a big question I’ll have to think about it for a second
I‘m thinking about lots of people right now like politicians, educators and all sorts
Educators like Confucius sets the tone of the Chinese cultural identity, like being polite, respecting old people
So great thinkers and educators can influence generations after generations, so people would respect them a lot

The important are like , well, they taught the Chinese traditional virtues like respecting elderly and others.
