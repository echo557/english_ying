Education

教育的对象/阶段—教育的方式—教育的目的

教育的目的 the purposes of education

【题库真题】
Some people think that teachers should be responsible for teaching students how to judge right and wrong and how to behave well.
Some say that teachers should only teach students about academic subjects. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

审题：
Some people think that teachers should be responsible for teaching students how to judge right and wrong and how to behave well.
Some say that teachers should only teach students about academic subjects. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

be responsible for某人该做某事

only
极端不成立法则
too much, never, best, must …

教育的阶段/对象：
students: school / general education
教育的方式：
teachers = school education / schooling: teachers + peers
教育的目的 the purposes of education：
1) teaching students how to judge right and wrong and how to behave well
素质教育
values and attitudes
behaviors
misbehaviors
poor/vile behaviors
2) academic knowledge (academic subjects)
academic performance 成绩

具象化思维：
结合现实生活讨论（充实论证内容）
结合学校出现的问题: focus mainly on academic performance
结合当前青少年的问题: be self-centered, be indifferent to others,misbehaviors(bully), violent behaviors,potential young criminals

素质教育：
moral education
how to judge right and wrong and how to behave well内容

教育方法

【题库真题】
Some people think parents should teach children how to be good members of society. Others, however, believe that school is the place to learn this. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

审题：
Some people think parents should teach children how to be good members of society. Others, however, believe that school is the place to learn this. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

the approach/method/fashion of education

  1. parenting/upbringing n. 家庭教育
    A good parent should help children (to) know what is important in life, what role they will play in their world, and how they are expected to behave.(the purposes/aims of education家庭教育的目的)

  2. schooling n. 学校教育
    Teachers should help children (to) learn how to cooperate with others and how to contribute to the life of their community.(the purposes/aims of education学校教育的目的)

科技对教育的影响

【题库真题】
Schooling is no longer necessary, since more information is accessible on the Internet, and students can study just as well at home. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

审题：
Schooling is no longer necessary, since more and more information is accessible on the Internet, and students can study just as well at home. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

极端不成立法则：
is no longer necessary

两种教育方式的对比：
schooling vs. the Internet

中心观点：
The purposes of school education cannot be fulfilled by the Internet.

主要论证内容：
学校的作用：
The main purposes of general education are teaching students how to judge right and wrong
and how to behave well (how to be good members of society) and teaching them about academic subjects.

网络的作用：
the role of the Internet：
searching for information, the development of independent learning and research skills

结论：
I think schools will continue playing the leading role in education, and the Internet will be a complementary part to it.

课外实践

【题库真题】
Young adults should undertake unpaid work to help people in the community.
Do you think it brings more drawbacks to the community and the young adults than benefits?

审题：
Young adults should undertake unpaid work to help people in the community. Do you think it brings more drawbacks to the community and the young adults than benefits?

讨论对象：
unpaid community service

从题目中整合出的重要思路：
Engaging in unpaid community work is a way to help students learn how to become good members of society.

【题库真题】
Some people believe that unpaid community work (eg. working in a charity organization; teaching sports to children)
should become a compulsory part of high school curriculum. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

审题：
Some people believe that unpaid community work (eg. working in a charity organization; teaching sports to children) should become a compulsory part of high school curriculum. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

极端不成立法则：
compulsory

中心观点：
Students should be encouraged to take the initiative to get involved in unpaid community service.
