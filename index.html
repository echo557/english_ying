好处-原因（实际好处/情绪）
Adv/important: able to make rational choices; being persistence-won’t give up easily;
can handle pressure well
Example: children/at work place/at school

基础答法：直接回应+抽象/具象扩展

  • Absolutely! Coz it helps people to make rational decisions when they meet problems, or it can help people to develop
    a persistent character.
  • Absolutely! Like for people who are patient at work, they can handle problems better coz they won’t get angry easily;
    for students who’re patient they can also get higher grades because they’re patient enough to deal with challenges.

    with challenges.

1

In some countries, increasing number of parents choose to educate their children at home by themselves instead of sending them to school.
Do the advantages of home education outweigh the disadvantages? 家庭教育与学校教育

审题：family education vs school education
分析：
instead of 这个地方不对

  1. 可以先让步，写family education得一些好处
    增进亲子关系
    更专注教育

  2. 写family education 的不足，只能通过学校教育才能做到
    但是无法学到合作
    也无法得到社会认可的学历
    可能无法融入集体

学校教育不仅是教知识
还教与人相处
全面发展自身
become good member of society

结论：
这种做法弊大于利， 小孩还是要上学，家庭教育取代不了学校教育

The better educational condition nowadays makes it possible for children to study not only at schools, and there are increasing numbers of
parents prefer their children to study at home and teach by themselves. There are many advantages that may bring to children, however
there is a wide concern that this phenomenon may have detrimental effects on their future development.

To begin with, one of multiple merits of teaching children at home is that parents can protect their children from bully. School violence is
common in school and enormous students have been bullied by others. Impact of bully leaves adverse influence on development of personality .
However, parents are able to eschew this miserable thing of their children by teaching them at home.
Moreover, by teaching children themselves, parents have chance to impose influence on their kids and enhance family bonds.
Children may imitate bad behaviors from classmates in school, teaching at home would avoid this problem and kids will learn to behave well from
parents, which can also enhance the relationship between family members.

However, teaching children at home has demerits. One of them is that teaching requires professional skills. Parents may have enough knowledge to
teach their own children, while their teaching skills are not as mature as qualified teachers in school. Furthermore, children study at home may lose chances
to company with young peers.Cooperating skills and communication skills are quite important to people when they become society members. Parents may
be able to teach children these skills’ theory at home, but without applying in practice, there is no help for children’s future development.
At last, enormous time is needed for teaching. As a matter of fact, parents do not have sufficient time to teach their children, due to they have to work.

To sum up, teaching children at home has advantages and disadvantages. However, compared with two sides, it seems that disadvantages overweight advantages.
School is better place for kids to study and parents education is only a supplementary method.

writing2019/1/21 -- Culture

Topic:
Some people think that young people should follow traditional of society, while others think
young people should be free to behave as they will. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

Analyze:
A:
Take care of older
Getting together in Spring Festival
Traditional holidays
Respecting and following conventional customs can enhance family cohesion.
Strengthen family bonds
sense of belonging to a family
reunite
B:
Bond young people’s lifestyles
outdated; obsolete
considerate
traditional virtue
obey sb’s instructions
uphold/maintain the tradition
disseminate
carry forward

Begin:
Tradition, young people
Be passed on
Preserve,maintain
Abandon the tradition
Represent a country’s culture
Affect people’s lifestyles and behaviors
Be a part of a country’s culture

Version1: Many traditions have been passed on for a very long time and even become an important part of a country’s culture.
Version2: Many traditions have been passed on for a very long time, but it seems that they are abandoned by young people today.
Version3: It is generally believe that traditions represents a country’s culture and affect people’s lifestyles and behaviors.

Version1: There is a debate as to whether young people should follow these traditions or not.
Version2: People have conflicting views about whether young people should follow these traditions or not.
Version3: Some people hold the opinion that young people should be required to follow traditions, while others think otherwise.
Version4: Some people believe young people should be required to follow traditions, which can benefit themselves and society as a whole.

Following conventional customs is a =n effective way to carry forward and disseminate traditional culture. For example, by reading
classical works of literature and wearing traditional costumes, young people will feel pound of the splendid culture created by their ancestors
and meanwhile spread the culture to other countries.
Respecting and following conventional customs can enhance family cohesion. A good example is that, on Chinese spring festival
young people are expected to return home to reunite with their family members, no matter how busy they are. They can help their parents wash dishes and clean the room.
This can give young people a sense of belonging to a family, which is important to their wellbeing and the stability of society.

(They can have quality time with their parents, cooking meals and watching TV programs together.)

However, some people argue that young people should be allowed to behave in their own way, as they live in a world that is different from the past.
Some traditions are outdated and not appropriate in the modern society, should be abandoned. For instance, in Chinese traditional culture,
a person has to obey his parents’ instructions in his career and marriage. This goes against the value of the
modern society and can impede(affect) individuals’ development.

Personally, I do not agree that young people should follow traditions in every aspect of their lives. It is reasonable for them to maintain some traditions and
customs that are still valuable in today’s society, while some obsolete ones should be abandoned.
