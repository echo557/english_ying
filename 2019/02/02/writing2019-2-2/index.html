扩充写作词汇

问题：

lack sth

介词+名词
一个句子不能用两个从句

方法：

  1. 通过范文背词
  2. 词汇总结
  3. 话题素材再写

    提升造句能力

    (语法准确，句子的扩展，培养语感)

Many students get addicted to video games and lack physical exercise, which may cause various health problems

When people drive cars, they need to concentrate on the road in order to avoid possible
accidents and using mobile phones is strictly forbidden.

When people drive cars, they need to concentrate on the road in order to avoid possible
accidents and any behavior that may distract their attention is strictly forbidden.

If one drives a car, they have to concentrate on the road (剑桥12-1)

Wasting water is a serious problem in farms, factories and households due to aging of facilities order
the lack of supervision.

段落论证扩展

政府应该控制用水（为什么，怎么控制， 结果怎样）
水短缺， 水浪费， 政府有义务控制

提高水价， 倡导节水， 推广节水技术

水的消耗减少，达到预期，可持续发展

Fresh water is valuable resource, while in many areas, water shortage is a serious problem.

Fresh water is valuable resource, while many areas, particular some remote areas, are short of water for natural and geographical reasons.
