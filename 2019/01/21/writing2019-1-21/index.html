writing2019/1/21 -- Lecture & Example (Culture)

Topic:
Some people think that young people should follow traditional of society, while others think
young people should be free to behave as they will. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

Analyze:
A:
Take care of older
Getting together in Spring Festival
Traditional holidays
Respecting and following conventional customs can enhance family cohesion.
Strengthen family bonds
sense of belonging to a family
reunite
B:
Bond young people’s lifestyles
outdated; obsolete
considerate
traditional virtue
obey sb’s instructions
uphold/maintain the tradition
disseminate
carry forward

Begin:
Tradition, young people
Be passed on
Preserve,maintain
Abandon the tradition
Represent a country’s culture
Affect people’s lifestyles and behaviors
Be a part of a country’s culture

Version1: Many traditions have been passed on for a very long time and even become an important part of a country’s culture.
Version2: Many traditions have been passed on for a very long time, but it seems that they are abandoned by young people today.
Version3: It is generally believe that traditions represents a country’s culture and affect people’s lifestyles and behaviors.

Version1: There is a debate as to whether young people should follow these traditions or not.
Version2: People have conflicting views about whether young people should follow these traditions or not.
Version3: Some people hold the opinion that young people should be required to follow traditions, while others think otherwise.
Version4: Some people believe young people should be required to follow traditions, which can benefit themselves and society as a whole.

Following conventional customs is a =n effective way to carry forward and disseminate traditional culture. For example, by reading
classical works of literature and wearing traditional costumes, young people will feel pound of the splendid culture created by their ancestors
and meanwhile spread the culture to other countries.
Respecting and following conventional customs can enhance family cohesion. A good example is that, on Chinese spring festival
young people are expected to return home to reunite with their family members, no matter how busy they are. They can help their parents wash dishes and clean the room.
This can give young people a sense of belonging to a family, which is important to their wellbeing and the stability of society.

(They can have quality time with their parents, cooking meals and watching TV programs together.)

However, some people argue that young people should be allowed to behave in their own way, as they live in a world that is different from the past.
Some traditions are outdated and not appropriate in the modern society, should be abandoned. For instance, in Chinese traditional culture,
a person has to obey his parents’ instructions in his career and marriage. This goes against the value of the
modern society and can impede(affect) individuals’ development.

Personally, I do not agree that young people should follow traditions in every aspect of their lives. It is reasonable for them to maintain some traditions and
customs that are still valuable in today’s society, while some obsolete ones should be abandoned.
