Love City

What it is famous for:
–Chengdu, is famous for its low speed living environment, comfortable city to live and foodies heaven.
It has so many wide-famous attractions, like, hot pat, pandas, Sichuan local plays, majiang, and so on
It’s just a charming and awesome city. If you like city lives as well, there also have many bars you can
choice, like the most famous bar in Chengdu named Languifang.

Why you think it’s a beautiful city：
–I love Chengdu and I really happy to live here. Chengdu is so beautiful and vibrant at the same time.
This city is always something going on in the city, it’s a city that never sleep.And it has amazing restaurants
here, which is such a big attraction for a foodie like me. And moreover, I like the culture and lifestyles here.
Suitable, low speed living lives, hospitable citizens are made feel comfortable, it’s a city kept its open house.
I guess that’s why I lived here for so many years. So, that’s the beautiful city I like, thanks for listening.

Where it is and how you knew it：

–As for where Chengdu is, well, I guess everybody knows about the city, it’s the provincial city of Sichuan province.
It’s located in the middle southwest of China. I study and work here for nearly 10 years, I know it from my parents
.They say Chengdu is a big city where is fun and have many opportunities. It’s a good place to achieve my dream.

Do you think having too many tourists is a positive thing for historical attractions?

–It’s definitely not a positive thing. I mean, I know some people may say that having tons of tourists is a good thing
for the frame of the attraction, and also it brings good income to the area, and part of money can be used to preserve
the attraction. But, when it comes to “too many” tourists, it becomes a burden. Especially for historical sites, they
are so fragile, too many people will damage the place

Books

A books you read

– Well, I want to describe a book, I recently read whose name is “How to read a book”. I don’t know who has written it.
I knew it from one of my best friend, she has recommended it to me after she had read it. She sayed the book is very helpful
for teaching people to read different kinds of reading materials. Well, the book shares some methods for people to read. It contains
: Firstly, reading levels, including basic reading and scanning reading, we need to learn how to become a clever reader.
Secondly, the third reading level. People learn to analyze reading, including to learn the deep meaning from the book,
how to judge a book.how to read fictions or literary books. Thirdly,how to read different kinds of books, the tool books,
literary books, science books or magazines. The last one, is how to get themes and artistic of the books
