sedentary lifestyle 久坐的生活方式

encourage sb to do sth; encourage doing
suggest that sb should do sth;suggest doing

There are increasing numbers of car accidents happening in China because the drivers use their phones when they are driving.

Many young people tend to stay up late at night, while their parents,who have totally different timetable,may be disturbed and suffer from sleep insufficiency.

As students suffer from high stress in study, some of them cannot fall asleep at night and they worry that they may fail the exam.

Due to the high stress in study, some students cannot fall asleep at night, worrying that they may fail the exam.

I personally think that some people do have talents that are probably inherited via their genes. Such talents can give individuals a facility for certain skills that allow them to excel, while more hard-working students never manage to reach a comparable level.

The government has the obligation to intervene in people’s diet as unhealthy eating habits may lead to a serials of diseases such as diabetes,high blood pressure, obesity,etc. and increase the expenditure on(its budget allocated to) medication(medicine). The authorities need to take measures to change people’s lifestyles and promote healthy diet.
