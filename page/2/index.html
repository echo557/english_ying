列出考点： 原因/利弊/比较现象/具体经历
好处-原因（实际好处/情绪）
Adv/important: able to make rational choices; being persistence-won’t give up easily;
can handle pressure well
Example: children/at work place/at school

基础答法：直接回应+抽象/具象扩展

  • Absolutely! Coz it helps people to make rational decisions when they meet problems, or it can help people to develop
    a persistent character.
  • Absolutely! Like for people who are patient at work, they can handle problems better coz they won’t get angry easily;
    for students who’re patient they can also get higher grades because they’re patient enough to deal with challenges.

升级答法：回应+抽象观点+具象扩展

  • Absolutely! It brings many practical benefits to people’s life and work,. The biggest one is that it helps
    to make rational decisions. Like at work places, patient employees usually can tackle problems better coz they
    would get to the bottom of it; for students, they also can gain higher grades if they’re patient, as they would dealing
    with challenges.

chasing deadline or work over night

In some countries, increasing number of parents choose to educate their children at home by themselves instead of sending them to school.
Do the advantages of home education outweigh the disadvantages? 家庭教育与学校教育

审题：family education vs school education
分析：
instead of 这个地方不对

  1. 可以先让步，写family education得一些好处
    增进亲子关系
    更专注教育

  2. 写family education 的不足，只能通过学校教育才能做到
    但是无法学到合作
    也无法得到社会认可的学历
    可能无法融入集体

学校教育不仅是教知识
还教与人相处
全面发展自身
become good member of society

结论：
这种做法弊大于利， 小孩还是要上学，家庭教育取代不了学校教育

The better educational condition nowadays makes it possible for children to study not only at schools, and there are increasing numbers of
parents prefer their children to study at home and teach by themselves. There are many advantages that may bring to children, however
there is a wide concern that this phenomenon may have detrimental effects on their future development.

To begin with, one of multiple merits of teaching children at home is that parents can protect their children from bully. School violence is
common in school and enormous students have been bullied by others. Impact of bully leaves adverse influence on development of personality .
However, parents are able to eschew this miserable thing of their children by teaching them at home.
Moreover, by teaching children themselves, parents have chance to impose influence on their kids and enhance family bonds.
Children may imitate bad behaviors from classmates in school, teaching at home would avoid this problem and kids will learn to behave well from
parents, which can also enhance the relationship between family members.

However, teaching children at home has demerits. One of them is that teaching requires professional skills. Parents may have enough knowledge to
teach their own children, while their teaching skills are not as mature as qualified teachers in school. Furthermore, children study at home may lose chances
to company with young peers.Cooperating skills and communication skills are quite important to people when they become society members. Parents may
be able to teach children these skills’ theory at home, but without applying in practice, there is no help for children’s future development.
At last, enormous time is needed for teaching. As a matter of fact, parents do not have sufficient time to teach their children, due to they have to work.

To sum up, teaching children at home has advantages and disadvantages. However, compared with two sides, it seems that disadvantages overweight advantages.
School is better place for kids to study and parents education is only a supplementary method.

For school children, their teachers have more influence on their intelligence and social development than their parents.

To what extent do you agree or disagree? 学校教育与其他教育方式

分析：
classes of teachers

Agree
学校教育教育方法
其他教育作为补充

The traditional mode of teaching-learning is experiencing changes in many schools, with various multiple-media tools are introduced to the
class. Some people claim that students in schools or university can learn more when compared with these media tools, I partly agree with idea.

There is no denying that videos and movies can reach the aim of imparting knowledge to students and compared with teaching in schools, these teaching
aids can effectively attract children’s attention and make the process of learning more enjoyable. This is particularly important for those young children, who
have difficulty in concentration. However, learning from classes does not necessarily mean the class is boring and there are various ways to maintain learners’ enthusiasm,
such as doing role-plays in the class or adding interaction parts with teachers.

My arguments advocating universities also lie in other two aspects. Firstly, learning methods gives students more from teachers. Teachers have a lot of teaching
students how to directly get effective methods to get knowledge, while learning from medias needs students themselves to figure out the learning ways. For those
students who have weaker ability to find ways to learn, teachers is the wiser choice compared with other resources. Another advantage of university is it provide
a real environment for students to know how to communicate and cooperate with other people. When use other resource to learning,they have no chance to improve communication skills
and the cooperation skills with persons face to face. They also may lead to worse influence on their future careers, from classes and teachers children
could prepare these skills to help them to achieve their goal in work place.

Overall, other resources such as TV and Internet can be adopted in classroom to assist teachers’ work. However, teachers can teach students more as they
can achieve better result in learning methods and contribute to children’s future development.
