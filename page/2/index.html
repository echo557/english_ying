Many students get addicted to video games and lack physical exercise, which may cause various health problems

accidents and using mobile phones is strictly forbidden.

accidents and any behavior that may distract their attention is strictly forbidden.

speaking2019/2/1 - Vocabulary

Do you think it’s important to be patient?-人群具体化
列出考点： 原因/利弊/比较现象/具体经历
好处-原因（实际好处/情绪）
Adv/important: able to make rational choices; being persistence-won’t give up easily;
can handle pressure well
Example: children/at work place/at school

基础答法：直接回应+抽象/具象扩展

  • Absolutely! Coz it helps people to make rational decisions when they meet problems, or it can help people to develop
    a persistent character.
  • Absolutely! Like for people who are patient at work, they can handle problems better coz they won’t get angry easily;
    for students who’re patient they can also get higher grades because they’re patient enough to deal with challenges.

升级答法：回应+抽象观点+具象扩展

  • Absolutely! It brings many practical benefits to people’s life and work,. The biggest one is that it helps
    to make rational decisions. Like at work places, patient employees usually can tackle problems better coz they
    would get to the bottom of it; for students, they also can gain higher grades if they’re patient, as they would dealing
    with challenges.

Why some people say being patient is a kind of virtue in modern life?
(升级答法： 回应+抽象观点/具象扩展)
Modern life - why not patient - agitated 焦躁 -fast pace of life - stressful life
Benefits of being patient-make rational decisions
Eg: teacher-custom service-bank- doctor

cloud people’s judgment: 影响人们的判断
chasing deadline or work over night
get agitated
see things through
come up with a good solution

  • Well I guess being patient can help people to make rational decisions, as the high pressure of life often cloud people’s judgment.
  • Many works would require people to stay patient under pressure like when chasing deadlines or working over night. So when people meet
    problems if they get agitated they would make the wrong choices. but they’re patient enough, they would be able to see things through and
    come up with a good solution.
  • 例子 Financial analyst/computer scientist- reward handsomely
