grammar-及物动词与不及物动词的区别

动词分成及物动词与不及物动词.

及物动词： 字典里词后标有vt. 的就是及物动词.及物动词后必须跟有动作的对象（即宾语）,并且可直接跟宾语.
如see 看见 (vt.) +宾语 I can see a boy.
其实所谓“及物”,就是后面可直接加宾语的动词,有被动形式,

不及物动词是没有被动式的,也不可直接加宾语,需加上介词. vi

及物动词后面可直接接宾语,不及物动词后面不可直接接宾语,一般要加介词后再接宾语.实际上很多动词既是及物动词,又是不及物动词.我举一个例子,就说write.如I am writing.和I am writing a letter.在前一个句子write是不及物动词,在后一个句子write是及物动词.又如,see是及物动词,但在特殊情况下如seeing is believing.

不及物动词就是一个动作不能施加到另一个物体上,也就是后面不能加宾语.
例如：He is running. run这个动词就是不及物动词,后面不能加sth.（不能说跑什么东西）

分清及物不及物动词:
动词及物与不及物通常有以下几种情况：

a．主要用作及物动词.及物动词后面必须跟宾语.可以用于：”主＋谓＋宾”；”主＋谓＋双宾”；”主＋谓＋宾＋宾补”结构.如：
He reached Paris the day before yesterday.
Please hand me the book over there.
They asked me to go fishing with them.
类似的还有：buy, catch, invent, found, like, observe, offer, prevent, promise, raise, find, forget, receive, regard, see, say, seat, supply, select, suppose, show, make, take, tell.

b．主要用作不及物的动词.不及物动词后面不跟宾语.只能用与：”主＋谓”结构.
This is the room where I once lived.
类似的还有：agree, go, work, listen, look, come, die, belong, fall, exist, rise, arrive, sit, sail, hurry, fail, succeed.

c．既可以用作及物又可以用作不及物的动词,其意义不变.如begin 都是作”开始”讲.
everybody , our game begins. let us begin our game.
类似的还有：start, answer, sing, close, consider, insist, read, learn, prepare, pay, hurt, improve.

d．既可以用作及物又可以用作不及物的动词,其意义完全不同.
这类动词作不及物动词是一个意义；而作及物动词时却是另一个意义.

如lift作不及物动词时是指烟雾的”消散”.
we saw the mountain when the clouds lifted.

作及物动词时是”升高；举起”.
He lifted his glass and drank.

类似的还有：
beat vi.跳动 vt. 敲、打;
grow vi.生长 vt. 种植
play vi.玩耍 vt. 打（牌、球）,演奏
smell vi.发出（气味） vt. 嗅
ring vi.（电话、铃）响vt.打电话
speak vi.讲话 vt. 说（语言）
hang vi. 悬挂 vt. 绞死
operate vi.动手术 vt. 操作

在英语错误中,“及物动词＋介词＋宾语”（transitive verb+preposition+object）,是常见的一种.
所谓及物动词,就是谓语动词（predicative verb）,不必通过介词引荐宾语.

相反的,不及物动词（intransitive verb）是不带宾语的.
有许多动词,虽然性质是及物的,但不一定要有宾语,如下列的①a和②a便是这种情形：
①a. We study every day.
b. Do you study English every day.

②a. Please write clearly next time.
b. Can you write your composition now?

如果本质上就是不及物动词,就不会有宾语；若要宾语,就要借介词之助,一起连用才行(不及物动词+宾语+介词),
如③b和④b;③a和④a是错的；

③a. The children are listening the music.
b. The children are listening to the music. ④a. She is laughing the crippled man.
b. She is laughing at the crippled man.

反之,及物动词不必靠介词,就可以带宾语,如上述的①b和②b ,又如⑤和 ⑥：
⑤ John is giving a book to me.
⑥ Who will answer this question?
如果无意中把介词加上,就错了,如：
*⑦ Who will answer to this question?
下列这句从房地产广告中看到的句子,也犯了同样的错：
“We have many buyers awaiting for available units here.”
“Awaiting”是个及物动词,后面的介词“for”是多余的,要去掉；不然把“awaiting”改为“waiting for”也行.
许多人习惯上喜欢把介词加到及物动词后面,然后才带出宾语.最常见的是“emphasize/stress on/upon”和“discuss about”,如：
⑧ Singaporeans seem to have emphasized on material gains.
⑨ In our education system, we stress upon examination results.
⑩ World leaders spent a lot of time discussing about worsening economic problems.
显然的,这三句里的介词“on/upon”和“about”是多余的,不必要的.
下面是些类似的错误：
● The young must obey to their elders.
● Do not approach to that odd-looking man.
● The audience attacked on the rude speaker.
● Nothing can escape from his parents’ eyes.
● Do you hope to serve for your nation?
● When did Susan marry with Paul?
介词“to, on, from, for, with”都要去掉才对.
为什么会有这些错误呢?主因是分不清楚及物动词和不及物动词的性质.其次,就是对同一个动词及其名词的句型有些混淆.解决之道有二.第一,要把“及物动词＋宾语”和“不及物动词＋介词＋宾语”划分清楚,如：
1） I did not answer him./ I did not reply to him.
2）He reached Londan yesterday./ He arrived in London yesterday.
第二,把及物动词转化为名词,然后加上适当的介词和宾语,如：
1） Don’t approach such a person.
2）Is oral practice a good approach to language teaching?

Writing Sentence(Exercise)

Family & City

  1. In the old times,it’s common to see that several family generations live in one house, this type of mode helps families live through starvation times and enhance family members’ cohesion.

  2. Due to the raising of citizens’ living standard, they are hunting for having more independent and more personal spaces than previous day.

  3. If people could burden to buy new houses or rent departments, they would prefer to build their own family and enjoy their private lifestyle.

  4. It is believed that convenient massive transportation and communication makes it possible for people to move frequently.

  5. Taking China for example, many young people move from rural area to urban city in order to find a good job and make a good living.

  6. With family structure change, it seems that family members bonds tend to weaker than previous days, which contribute to serials problems.

  7. On one aspect, the elderly need care and attention, but in fact it seems it’s hard for them to get that, due to their adult offspring are too busy with works or personal businesses.

  8. Without helping from parents, those young parents from coin family need to tackle with all living problems by themselves, especially taking care of their next generation.

  9. Core family tends to become popular family mode in society nowadays, but we also need to pay attention to its possible side effects of this trend.

  10. In many countries, family’s scale is decreasing due to more and more people are likely to live alone or be single.

Education

  1. It is believed that, the purpose of education is to cultivate people as good members for society.

  2. In many countries,government would afford parts of expenditure or assist fund for schools.

  3. People look forward to graduates could make contributions to society.

  4. Well-educated people could well serve society via knowledges and skills from education.

  5. People believe that the purpose of education is to cultivate ability person for the development of society.

  6. From the point view of students, the purpose of education is to realize their dreams and ambitions.

  7. There is no denying that no all countries are able to provide free and high quality compulsory education for children.

  8. If people could aware of that hard study in schools would let them contribute to better life in the future, they would pay more enthusiasm in studies.

  9. There are two purpose of education, one is prosperity of society, the other is realizing personal dreams.

  10. If we overemphasized the benefits bring to society via education, people may lose the study enthusiasm.

Career

  1. In today’s knowledge age society, science and techniques are developing rapidly.

  2. The first cause is that science is more difficult than other subjects, which means students could achieve science degree unless they invest more efforts.

  3. Becoming a chemist or physicist generally need students widen their theory courses and spend many years working in laboratory.

  4. Because the process is long and boring, it may prevent many young people to select science as their major.

  5. It seems that science major graduates are unpopularity in job market.

  6. They may have professional basis of theoretical knowledge, but this can not satisfy employer’s job requirements, due to employer prefer to hire employees with practical skills.

  7. Compared with math major students, a graduate who has accounting certificates is seemed more easily to find jobs.

  8. Because people are becoming more and more realistic, it’s no doubt that enrollment rate of science students is decreasing gradually.

  9. It is well-known to people, society’s productive power rely on the development of techniques,
    and in turn technological developing is built upon scientific basic theory research.

  10. If there were not sufficient people doing science research, technological creation will be prevented, country would lose its competition in the long term.

Criminal

  1. It seems that crimes can not be estimated, due to it’s existed in every country, whatever we should make great efforts to fight it.

  2. In order to estimate crimes, we have to find out the root causes and solutions.

  3. In some emerging nations, gap between rich and poor is extending rapidly, which is caused various economical and violent crimes occur.

  4. Gap between rich and poor is still the main criminal cause in some regions.

  5. Research shows due to genes and growing environment, a portion of people have antisocial characters in the country, but it’s hard to recognize and prevent them doing crazy behaves.

  6. In the contrary, there are many measures to reduce crimes effectively.

  7. These heavy crimes must be punished hardly, so that the people who want to do crimes can be terrified.

  8. Due to many criminal behaviors are caused by people who are in shortage of law conscious or hard to survive, we need to provide education for prisoners and teach them practical skills during their prison time.

City problems

  1. Because urban population is too many and the land resource is limited, which lead to living house shortage problems.

  2. If some companies and staffs moved to countryside, the requirements of living house in urban cities would be reduced, as a result house pricing would be declined.

  3. The number of people take cars go to work in cities will be reduced,so as the traffic conjunctions.

  4. The advantage of moving company to countryside is not only developing local economy but also reducing gap between countryside and city.

  5. People need waste a lot time on the way to work place that may make them exhausted and have bad mood.

  6. However,strategy of transferring companies to countryside may bring some problems.

  7. Employees may not want to move to rural areas, because once they do it, they are no longer able to enjoy high quality education resources, medical services and entertainment facilities in cities.

  8. The infrastructures in countryside are relatively backward than city’s and the lives are inconvenient either.

Science

  1. There is no denying that computer technology promote social productive forces rapidly.

  2. People are no longer need to go to library to look for reference books but to get information via online database.

  3. Using emails and mobile phones make it possible for people cooperating and communicating with each other is more effective and convenient.

  4. Other people are worried about that techniques may make things more complicated and inconvenient.

  5. Taking genetically modified food for example, their safety is uncertainty yet so they may bring some health risks to humans.

  6. Using massive agricultural machinery and chemical fertilizer gain corn’s production, which provide sufficient food for growing populations.

  7. Radiation generated by electronic devices would be harmful to people’s health, what’s more there’s no effective methods to avoid it.

  8. There is another controversial problem is that with widely usage of chemical fertilizer and pesticides, its remaining chemical substance would pollute soil and underground water.

  9. I believe that humans’ better future depends on science and technology’s further development.

  10. If we could pay more attention to environmental protection and human health when pursuing advanced technology then technology was able to serve human better.

题干题

  1. 属于顺序题
  2. 如果一个Section 出现两个自然段，重点看第二自然段首句
  3. 如果一个section 出现多个自然段，如果没有时间，重点看第一个自然段

####　判断题
1.　一般都是顺序题

  1. 如果判断题题干将全文主旨分为多个步骤或者阶段，直接在原文查找具体的步骤或者阶段数字， 从文章后面想前查找
  2. be 后方通常为考点 , 也是定位词 virtually totally 几乎完全
  3. 举例： 原文：当讲题的时候xx不会做题。 题干：当讲题之前，xx 不会做题。 True
  4. 当almost 出现时，70%概率选择Yes
  5. 举例： 原文： 赵同学家里有一个美丽的妹妹。 题干： 赵同学的家，由他，他爸爸，他妈妈构成。
  6. consist of 在题干出现时，80%概率选择NO
  7. 题干最长的时候，选择Yes 的概率在70%+
  8. 动词的后方信息为考点
  9. 首段尾句是问句，否定前方已知信息，并且引出下文
  10. 题干明显比别人短的，选NG 的概率不大

匹配题

  1. 大概率是顺序题

单选题

  1. 顺序题
  2. 对选项做positive， negative 以及中性判断
  3. 对比选项分类

人们大量购买家电产品

In many countries, more and more people buy a wide range of household goods like television, microwave oven and rice cooker. Do you think this is a positive or negative development? 人们大量购买家电产品

分析：positive
分析：

  1. Positive: Provide entertaining method, save time and energy
  2. Electronic waste, 浪费电，占用空间，

Nowadays there is an increasing number of people are fond of buying household electrical appliances, such as television, microwave oven and rice cooker. This phenomenon both have advantages and disadvantages, and I think it has more positive effects than negative effects.

There is no denying that too many electrical devices would waste a lot of electric power, especially for those appliances which need power supply continuously, such as air conditions and refrigerators.These wastes are occurred approximately in every family. Furthermore, people really like to buy kinds of appliances, especially when new patterns come out, residents would like to replace the old ones as new ones. That is not only led to waste money but also generate new rubbishes, which is not friendly to the environment.

However, the positive development of buying household can not be ignored. Buying households by people may represent citizens are more care about their life quality than before, in old days many people are worrying about survival so they couldn’t afford to other things except food. In addition, appliances provide a life easy way for nowadays’ citizen, with supported by high technology, people’s life are more convenient and comfortable. Economy will be increased by people’s consume, our society is built on economical currents, consumers’ purchasing is essential part. When people would like to afford appliances means this country’s economy is in a good condition.

To sum up, although some people are arguing that buying wide range of households may bring power waste and environmental harmful, advanced technology may resolve these problems. In my opinion, I think buying households is a positive development.

在线会议、培训

As technology develops, online business meetings and business training increasingly rise. Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages? 在线会议、培训

分析： advantages are more than disadvantages
advantages：不限制时间和空间， 节省出差成本。可以锻炼会议组织者的能力。 多方参与。

disadvantages： 会议或者培训果没有面对面的高； 需要参会者高度集中，考验会议技巧； 设备上支持花钱，对于会议和培训少的公司是一种浪费

Technology has changed the way we worked and it has bought some fan at work,it reduces on human errors which can be caused by too much work or stress. Business technology like online business meetings and training are tend to more and more popular. They have both advantages and disadvantages,in this topic I will analyze them briefly.

At first, the advantage of online business is remarkable,the tools make it possible for people are no long limited by time and locations.Nowadays staffs from different cities or countries can attend meeting or training by Internet communication tools,which must need people to have travels by air or trains in previous day. Without frequently traveling,budget can be saved, money can be spent on somewhere else such as staffs.
At second,online communication tools make companies to have deep collaboration to come true,especially for transnational cooperations.Telephone meetings,skype conferences make people have access to make business decisions regardless of daytime and nighttime.

On one hand,there is no denying that online meetings have disadvantages. Many network conferences have poor performances,compared with face to face meetings, it’s more difficult to draw audiences’ attention . In order to accomplish conferences with high performance,organizers need to prepare high quality slides,have smoothly expression and use easy understanding and logical words. Moreover, audiences must be concentrate on the meetings can get all the information from it. In addition,travel expenditure may be saved but communication tools would spend a lot of money, companies also need take equipment’s maintenance into budget.
