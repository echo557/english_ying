Purpose

Schooling
Teaching students how to achieve academic knowledge.
Teachers should help children to learn how to cooperate with others and how to contribute to the life of their community.
Parenting
Teaching students how to judge right and wrong and how to behave well in society.
A good parent should help children to know what is important in life, what role they will play in their world, and how they excepted
to behave.
Community
Students should be encouraged to take the initiative to get involved in unpaid community services.
Study in group
Studying in group make students know how to cooperate with each other and how to develop their each aspects of abilities.
Study alone
Studying alone increase students’ ability to have an independent mind and develop their initiative ability to learn by themselves.
Study with different talents
It is considered as an educational platform for students to develop their versatile skills and grow into a person who gets accustomed to the needs of the society.
Only focusing on academic skills inevitably leads many who are short of academic study but good at other subjects to lose opportunities to promote their talents.
In addition, if students with different abilities can study together, it is out of question that all the students will benefit from such a combination.
This is simply because it helps students complement each other and give them motivation as well as chances to learn more from each other.

Topics1

Lectures were used as a way of teaching large numbers of students, but now with the development of technology for education, many people think there is no justification for attending lectures.
To what extent do you agree or disagree? 上课

审题： 科技+Education
no justification: 需要去做否定
观点：No justification to attend lectures with the development of technology

2019/1/20 20:31

Nowadays, rapid development of technology changes many aspects of human living, including educational methods. People are arguing about whether attending letters is necessary or not with the bring
advanced technology. I firmly believe that going to college or university and attending lectures is still important.

It is undeniable that Internet makes it possible for those people who are living in remote places or needing to work in daylight to have access to study. Without wasting time and energy in transportation,
people could learn in anywhere and anytime they want. Due to Internet and computers, those people can fulfill their study desires.

However, technology cannot replace the importance of learning in classroom. Comparing with attending virtual classes, going to reality classrooms is still the main method for students to acquire knowledge.
Students can not only gain knowledge in class, but also can make good interaction with teachers. Teachers can provide a faster and simper way to present information to students, they can come down to the level
of a student and so are definitely better than computers. Furthermore, computers only provide a limitation way in one class and can not simulate all the situations when students attend it. In the aspect,
attending lectures would have more benefit, a good teacher can change their teaching methods in classes according to students. In addition, more than academic subjects students can gain, social skills and communication
skills also can be learned.

In conclusion, I think attending lectures will continue playing the leading role in education, and development of technology will be a complementary part to it.

Topic 2

Some people believe that people who read books can develop more imagination and language skills than those who prefer to watch TV. To what extent do you agree or disagree? 读书是否比看电视更能培养孩子想象力和语言技能

审题：
develop more imagination and language skills
A: read books
更专注
只有文字，唯有调动想象力才能读出书里的精妙

B: watch TV
relaxation way
imagination only restricted by pictures and images
not oral language
Begin去找切入点

Watching TV is a common popular way for people to spend their leisure time, with various programs available on the screen 24 hours a day. There is a viewpoint that those who prefer reading books to watching TV can become
more imaginative and capable use of languages. I completely agree with this point and give extent.

First of all, compared with watching TV,reading books give people more room to imagination. When we read the description of one scenery, we need to call our visualization in minds. As people have different understanding
of words, the pictures in our minds are different person to person. By contrast, TV only display one fixing pictures, we only see what we see. Actually most of the time, our imagination is restricted by pictures presented to us.

Moreover, reading can contribute to the development of people’s language skills, particularly for children. When we watching TV, we are only focus our attention on images and sounds, paying less attention on language skills.
Besides, most of the language on TV is oral style, not as formal and precise as the language on books. Those children who are addicted to watching TV and unwilling to reading more to improve their language skills, which lead to
there maybe too many spelling and grammatical mistakes in their writing.

In conclusion, watching TV is a good way for relaxation, but we should encourage people to do more reading for better development of their imagination and language skills.

Topic 3

Some countries spend a lot of money preparing competitors to take part in major competitions such as Olympic Games or football World Cup. Some people say that it would be better to spend this money encouraging children
to take up sports from a young age. To what extent do you agree or disagree? 政府投资专业运动员与青少年体育教育

Human history is companied with plenty of sports competitions. Olympic Games and other countless games, which play an enormous role in our daily lives. In order to achieve good performance in competitions, government should invest
a lot money. However, some people are argue that it would better to spend more money on children physical education. I think both opinions are reasonable, government should balance the two aspects expenditure.

On one hand, every single country wants to prove that they are the best in the world. There are multiple ways to prove this and sport is one of them.
Each year various international competitions take place around the globe. Furthermore, taking part in sports competitions and winning in them can make people feel proud of their nationalities. In addition, people could be encouraged to
join in physical training after inspired by their athletes winning. For example, there are some surveys report that people are willing to swimming during the period of international swimming competitions.
In order to standout in games, high professional coaches and well performed training facilities and place are indispensable, which need to cost plenty of money, and without doubts the expenditure needs governments’ support.

On the other hand, government should also make the budget on children’s physical education. Facilities need to be constructed, training should be provided and holding more games for youth. In this way
a nation maintain an energetic and efficient young generation to guarantee a sustainable development of the country.

In conclusion, I think the government should make a balance between the two. Physical activities for children is essential for maintaining a healthy and energetic generation.
Proper investment in world competition could help to prove the country itself on the international stage.

Represent Highest and Lowest Points in graphs

Highest:

  1. Verb
    peaked/culminated/climaxed/reach the peak/ hit the peak/touch the highest point/reach the vertex/reach the apex
  2. Noun
    a(/the)peak, a(/the)pinnacle, /a(/the) vertex, the highest point, an(/the) apex, a (/the) summit, a (/the) top,
    a(the) acme, a(/the) zenith

Lowest

  1. Verb
    touch the lowest point/ get the lowest point/reached the nadir
  2. Noun
    the lowest point, the lowest mark, bottommost point, rock bottom point, bottommost mark, nadir, the all-time low,
    the lowest level,the bottom, rock-bottom

Example:
