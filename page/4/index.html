Part1

Do you often use a map?

  • Yeah I do. I don’t have a good sense of direction. I will get lost if I don’t
    have my google map with me. I could say that I rely on my GPS app to get to different
    places in the city. I can’t live without maps.

Who taught you to use a map?

-My parents taught me how to use a paper map who I was little. They trained me a lot by
letting me find the way myself, using a map. And the electronic maps nowadays are very easy
to handle, so just figured out myself.

Did you save money when you were young?

-I think I did, sometimes. I mean, when I was little, my parents would give me pocket money each week for
things like snacks. But that wouldn’t be enough if I want to buy something more expensive.
So， from time to time, I would put the extra money in my piggy bank and save the money for something bigger.

If someone wants to draw a painting of you, will you agree?

  • Uhmm, sure, why not, I mean, I might feel very surprised and flattered that someone
    wants to draw a painting of me, because I’m nobody. But I won’t say no to that. It’ll be really cool
    to have a portrait of myself.

Part3

What can people do to protect their equipment from technical problems?

-Firstly, you need to be very careful while using the equipment. Always follow the instruction. The more careful
you are, the longer it will last. And then, make sure you get the warranty for the equipment you buy,
so that even if it breaks, you will have technician take care the problem for you for free. So I guess,
those are the things you could do.

What is required to be a good public speaker?

  • In order to be a good public speaker, first, you need to have a beautiful voice, and the ability to speak
    clearly and articulate your thoughts, that’s the first thing to make people pay attention to you when you give the
    talk, and second, you need to be appealing to your audience, that is to say, you need to be charismatic (有魅力的)，
    expressive （善于表达的), passionate and motivational(有启发的), you have to be able to engage with your audience.

Why would people spend a lot of time learning one skill?

  • Uhmmm, people spend lots of time learning one skill because, firstly, it always requires a lot of time and effort to
    master one skill (拿下某项技能), you have to keep practicing: and secondly, it gives people a sense of achievement and
    satisfaction to spend much time and effort on one thing and eventually become an expert on this thing. Our society praise
    people like that . And finally, through a huge amount of practice, it might become a habit, and people spend tons of
    time on keeping habit.

New technologies and ways of buying and selling are transforming the
life of consumers. What is your opinion on the issue

确定话题范围： 时尚与购物类话题
确定讨论对象： online shopping

influence
Analyze the influence of

In many countries, buying products online has become a mainstream activity.
While some people have concerns towards this trend, I think that it leads to several desirable results.

The development of online shopping has meant that the Internet has become a
world-wide retail market: exotic foods, art works, rare books, adventure holidays and the
list is endless. Products that were once available to a few people living in big cities
can now be bought by those living in small towns.

Additionally, buyers can have more control over the process. Prices comparison websites make it
easier to find bargains. Shopping can be done at any time of the day or night, and
shoppers can browser for the product as long as they like without pressure from
sale assistants. This means that shoppers can potentially become more astute and knowledgeable
about the products that they are buying. Because of the greater competition involved in
trading within a large market, sellers may have to improve the quality of their products.

However, that is not means that shopping online brings about no problems. One of the biggest concerns is safety.
With the increasing virus threats ans well-trained hackers, online deals are well exposed to all kinds of risks and losses,
for example, the wide-spread problem of identity theft.Therefore, when making the most of the desirable changes brought by
technology, we should be aware of its potential downsides and find a way to minimize the
negative impacts of them.

Therefore, experience suggests that online shopping has transformed modern life.
Consumers today not only have wider access to goods but also have greater access information
and more control over how they shop.

me. Lugu Lake is located at the cross of Sichuan and Yunnan Province, it more than 600 kilometers
away from Chengdu, and it was about 40 kilometers long around the lake. We had heard that palce was
very beautiful, so we made plans to get there for holidays. We had stay there for 4 days, and spent one dat
to ride around the whole lake, then spent two day to have deep visit for that place, we took boats on the lake,
went to the small island, climbed the mountains there and watched the beautiful sky in the night.
The food there are quite delicious and cheap, the people there are friendly and hospitable. Skies are very blue.
no air pollution, it’s a good place for busy people in cities to loose pressures.

had known each other more more than 3 years. She got married at the begin of this month in Chengdu, actually in one bar restaurant.
I and three other classmates also been invited, and we had prepared a special program for her in the wedding.
And when the day came, we went to the restaurant, and one thing suprised me was that she was very beautiful, like a princess.
The decoration of wedding place was like a fairy tale. We sang in the wedding and as witnesses of their wedding.
I felt so happy to see her enter the marriage with her husband, it was significant day for her and we friends wish her best wishes.

Well, I want to describe one holiday is that the spring festival. The spring festival is a traditional festivals for Chinese people.
It always at January or Februry, and it was counted as Chinese traditional timing. Alway the people are went back from study place or
work place to home for gathering. Whole family stay together and enjoy the precious moment.
You know, most young people are leaving home to seek study or make a living, only Spring Festival can have long holidays. Family stay together

And parents or grandparents will give present to children to give wishes to them. And busy people also take their leisure to visit relatives or
friends. It’s a big day for Chinese people.
